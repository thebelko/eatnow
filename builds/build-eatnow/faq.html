Getting started

Where can I use Eatnow?

At the moment Eatnow Bot works everywhere. We are hoping to extend to other cities in the near future. Eatnow is only supported with US phone numbers on iOS.

How do I change my phone number?

If you would like to change your phone number, text STOP to 7-6917 (Eatnow). Log out of your account in the app and then create a new account with the new phone number.

How do I permanently delete my account?

Please e-mail support@foursquare.com to permanently delete your account and all data associated with it. Note that if you signed into Foursquare Marsbot with the same account you use on Foursquare City Guide or Foursquare Swarm, your account will be deleted and all information from all three apps will be deleted.

Additional Information

We are in early stages of testing Foursquare Marsbot in the wild. Download the app and sign up for the waitlist if you’d like to help try Foursquare Marsbot. We’ll start inviting people to meet Foursquare Marsbot in NYC and SF first, stay tuned!
